About

The Open Government Partnership (OGP) is a multilateral initiative that was launched in 2011 that provides a platform for domestic reformers to secure concrete commitments and make their governments more responsive to citizens. The OGP promotes the principles of transparency, accountability, citizen participation and innovation and so far 65 countries have profited from the OGP platform to make over 2,000 open government commitments.

The OGP Global Summit is the initiativeâ€™s largest gathering of open government practitioners from all over the world. It is a biannual event hosted by the Country Chair in turn. The Summit is the event where representatives of governments, academia, civil society and multilateral organizations get together in order to exchange experiences, best practices and major developments in open government. For this, it presents an opportunity to raise the profile of open government issues internationally and to explore how they link to some of the biggest challenges the world is facing.

The Mexico 2015 OGP Global Summit will focus on highlighting how the principles of Open Government can promote the implementation of the sustainable development goals of the Post 2015 Agenda. It will be an opportunity to address the challenges faced in the implementation of the open government principles to increase institutional responsiveness to issues that hinder the achievement of inclusive development.
