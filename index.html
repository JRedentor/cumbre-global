Openness for All

Enabling Sustainable Development

The Open Government Partnership Global Summit has become the largest celebration and gathering of practitioners of open government from all over the world. This forum promotes productive dialogue, interaction, and sharing between civil society representatives, government officials and reformers that are making their governments more transparent, responsive and accountable.

In 2015 the OGP Global Summit will be held in Mexico and it will focus on highlighting how the principles of Open Government can increase institutional responsiveness to social challenges and promote the implementation of the Sustainable Development Goals of the Post-2015 Development Agenda.
