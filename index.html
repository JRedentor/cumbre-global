Openness for All

Enabling Sustainable Development

The 2015 Open Government Partnership Global Summit is a space that promotes a productive dialogue among civil society representatives, government officials, reformers and practitioners from around the world. Furthermore, the Mexico Global Summit will be the ideal place to discuss how open government can become an enabler of the implementation of the Post-2015 Development Agenda that will be launched in September.
