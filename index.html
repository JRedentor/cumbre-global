About the summit

The Open Government Partnership Global Summit is a space that promotes productive dialogue and interaction between civil society representatives, government officials, reformers and practitioners from around the world.

In Mexico 2015 the event will focus on highlighting how the principles of Open Government can promote the implementation of the sustainable development goals of the Post 2015 Agenda to be launched in September this year. It will explore the ways in which open government principles can increase institutional responsiveness to issues that hinder the achievement of inclusive development.
